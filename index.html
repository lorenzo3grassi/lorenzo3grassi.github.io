Lorenzo Grassi

Lorenzo Grassi is an assistant professor in the Coding Theory and Cryptology group at the Eindhoven University of Technology in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science, and a consultant at Ponos Technology.

His research is supported by the ERC Starting Grant "SYMPZON - Getting SYMmetric CryPtography Out of its Comfort ZONe" (announcement), starting in February 2025.

His research interests include all aspects of cryptology, with main focus on the design and cryptanalysis of symmetric cryptography primitives.
